A Story
Baked In.

Natalie Blomer is Cincinnati born and raised and graduated from the University of Cincinnati with a degree in Entrepreneurship. Natalie has special memories of going to her local donut spot as a kid, which led her to start baking there in 2012.

Once she had seven years of sprinkles and fritters under her belt, she launched the Blossim Donuts food truck in 2019.

In 2021, her dream of a permanent storefront became a reality—the culmination of years of hard work, early mornings, and a deep passion for the industry.

UC Alumna

Blomer + Ossim Blomer is her dad's family name and Ossim is her mom's family name—blending the two together to bring you Blossim Donuts.
